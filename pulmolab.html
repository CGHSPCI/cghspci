Pulmonary Department provides a wide range of therapeutic and diagnostic respiratory services to patient with cardiopulmonary disorder which is the heart and the lungs.This department covers services that can treat, evaluate and maintain cardiopulmonary function manned by highly skilled Respiratory Therapist

Services Offered :
Diagnostic Procedure

1. Pulmonary Function Test-
        A test that can measure the volume, capacity and flow of air inside the lungs during maximal inhalation and expiration. Available with bronchodilator and without bronchodilator

2. Arterial Blood Gas- a diagnostic procedure that can measure the amount of gases such as oxygen and carbon dioxide in the blood

Therapeutic Procedure

1. Aerosol Therapy/ Nebulization Procedure
        The delivery of medication through inhalation. Aides in sputum induction and treatment of lung problems

2. Oxygen Therapy-

        provides supplemental oxygen using different low flow and high flow oxygen delivery system

3. Chest physiotherapy- Aide in the mobilization of sputum by doing vibration and percussion

4. Postural Drainage- Assist patient in positioning for airway clearance .

5. Mechanical Ventilation- monitoring and managing patient hooked to invasive procedure.

6. Weaning from mechanical ventilator- provide the criteria and assess patient if he can tolerate the procedure.

7. High Flow Oxygen Therapy- providing non invasive procedure delivering blended oxygen and compressed air to patient

8. BIPAP- Bilevel Positive Airway Pressure/ CPAP -Continous Positive Airway Pressure - a non invasive procedure that can treat patient with COPD, sleep apnea and ALS

19. Incentive Spirometer- Breathing exerciser for post extubation and post operative patient

10. Peak Expiratory Flow Rate- a handheld device that can measure the air inside the lungs with one forceful blow
