DEPARTMENT OF CLINICAL PATHOLOGY



MISSION

To continually engage in advancements in field of Medical Technology to provide best laboratory diagnosis. To pave a way for a scheme of continous improvement of Medical Technologist through innovative strategies of training and methodology. To actively and harmoniously work with various allied health professionals and institutions in the process of diagnosis, prevention and cure of patients within the vicinity.


VISION

COMMUNITY GENERAL HOSPITAL OF SAN PABLO CITY INC. Department of Clinical Pathology exist to provide accurate, timely, and reliable laboratory test examination results to aid in the diagnostics of different diseases and help promote general wellness among the residents of San Pablo City and nearby communities.


CORE

Competence, Professionalism, Honesty, Social Responsibility, Excellence, Integrity, Commitment
