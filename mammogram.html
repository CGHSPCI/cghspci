COMPANY FUTURE PLANS

As envisioned by the founders of the corporation, the main business will be marketing and provision of diagnostic machines and services sush as Mammography,Echocardiography, electro-encephalography and other sophisticated diagnostic tools found in the large Medical Centers in manila, Makati and Qeuzon City and other nearby towns providing the same. bBecause of regulatory requirements and the time the will be spent in the search and negotiations with foreign companies. The initial service will be a CTSCAN from GE. Eventualy the other tools, products and services will be launched from the second year of operation to compete with other established global companies and complement locally existing ones.


MISSION

The San Pabo City Diagnostics, Incorporated was formed to provide quality but affordable diagnostic products and services physicians can recommend with confidence and trust in pursuit of accurate and effective patient care and management at reasonable and compatible rates, while providing reasonable returns to its shareholders.


VISION

We will be the first locally managed diagnostic corporation owned and operated by physicians in and around San Pablo City area in ten years. we will be respected and recornized as a partner of local and regional medical practitioners, anda reliable provider of quality but affordable diagnostic and other laboratory services to local patients.
