about us


MISSION

Our mission is to continually engage in the introduction of modern medical infrastructure facilities and state of the art equipment managed by efficient, competent, and reliable staff for delivery of professional healthcare to the community. Also, to cooperate with various government agencies and instrumentalities in the promotion of healthcare services.

VISION

The Community General Hospital of San Pablo City, Inc., exists to provide the best medical care.

CORE

We are guided by our core values of compassion, ethics, efficiency, effectiveness, teamwork, responsibility, innovation, creativity, and flexibility.


COMPANY PROFILE

COMPANY NAME: COMMUNITY GENERAL HOSPITAL OF SAN PABLO CITY, INC. NATURE OF BUSINESS: HOSPITAL(TERTIARY) ADDRESS: C. COLAGO AVENUE, SAN PABLO CITY 4000

OVERVIEW

As San Pablo City``s biggest tertiary hospital the Community General Hospital, Inc., a tertiary hospital with a ninety nine (99) beds capacity authorized by DOH and PHIC is directed by its Board of Directrs, employs personel staff of about 168 and is supported by 433 cooperative stockholders. Most of its active medical staff and visiting practitioner are board certified or well-trained in their respective specialties, reflecting a dedication and commitment to provide the highest quality of care to San Pablo City patients. All three leadership groups of the hospital- the board, medical staff and administration, continously work together to identify and direct priorities of the hopital through the development and implementation of its strategic plan.

Community General Hospital of San Pablo City, Inc.
Our Hospital was established and opened for public on September 15, 1969, It was licensed by the Department of Health as a 50-bed private general hospital with acereditation from sss & GSIS to service its Medicare patients. The hospital is located on an area of about 7,825 square meters located along the National Hi-way in Bo. San Roque. It is about 250 meters from the San Pablo - Alaminos - Calauan Junction and about 1 km. From the center of the city poblacion. The hospital consisted of three buildings of strong materials (reinforced concrete, hollow blocks & etc,) of about 2,700 square meters.

BRIEF HISTORY
Forty years ago, the hospital was conceived and planned by group of US-trained physician from San Pablo City. It is borne out of disappointed due to the lack of adequately equipped hospital in the area where they can practice their expertise gained from their US training for the benefits of the community. The existing hospitals at that time were not adequately equipped, hence, serious patients were sent to manila. After several meetings and dialogues of the organizing physicians with several civic leaders of the city, it was decided to establish a hospital better equipped, hence, serious patients were sent to Manila After several meetings and dialogues of the organizing physicians with several civic leaders of the city, it was decided to establish a hospital better equipped than the other hospitals.
