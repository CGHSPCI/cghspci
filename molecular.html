molecular laboratory
At Community General Hospital San Pablo City, Inc. our newly established Molecular Laboratory is dedicated to cutting-edge diagnostics and research. Our lab is like a code studio for scientists and medical professionals, where they decode the secrets of genetics to improve patient care. This state-of-the-art facility is equipped with the latest technology for analyzing DNA, RNA, and proteins.

In this lab, we're like beginner programmers, continuously learning and developing new methods to unravel the mysteries of diseases and provide precise treatments. From COVID-19 testing to cancer research, we're on a mission to be full-stack experts in genomics and molecular biology.

Our team is passionate about pushing the boundaries of medical science, making sure your health is in safe hands. We're here to bring you the best in molecular diagnostics and a brighter, healthier future."
