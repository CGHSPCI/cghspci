PHYSICAL/OCCUPATIONAL THERAPY SERVICES

Therapeutic Exercises: These exercises are carefully designed to address your specific needs, whether it's improving range of motion, strengthening weak muscles, enhancing flexibility, or correcting muscle imbalances. Progress is closely monitored, ensuring that each exercise benefits patient's recovery.

Modalities: To manage pain, reduce inflammation, and promote healing, our therapists my utilize various PT modalities. These may include Therapeutic Ultrasound High Intensity Laser, Shockwave Therapy, Lumbar/Cervical Traction, Infrared Radiation, Electrical Stimulation, Hot Moist Pack, and Cold Procedures like lumbar and cervical tractions, all aimed at enhancing your comport and well-being.

Manual Therapy: Hands-on approach is a cornerstone of therapy. Manual therapy involves various hands-on techniques, including joint mobilization, stretching, and manual traction. These methods are employed to improve joint function and relieve discomfort, all while ensuring comport and safety throughout the process.
